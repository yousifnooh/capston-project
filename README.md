# capston-project

Objective:
- Investigate how weather conditions affect pollution levels.
- Assess the role of vegetation in air quality differences.
- Analyze temporal patterns (daily, monthly, seasonal) of pollution variation.
- Evaluate human activities contributing to high PM10 .


Tools:
Python, Excel, Power BI

Description:
Data cleaning, analysis, and visualization to extract insights.

Key Insights:
- Increase taxes on factories and power plants whose emissions exceed the allowed limits
- Promote the use of public transportation and set regulations on private vehicle ownership
- Clean roads regularly from dust, and enforce dust control measures
- limit outdoor activities during critical conditions,  such as covering construction sites as practiced in China
